About the Project

Our project harnesses over 120 years of historical climate data from the Indian Meteorological Department to reveal how temperature and rainfall patterns have evolved across India. Using advanced statistical methods and machine learning models, we pinpoint vulnerability hotspots and provide evidence-based insights for policymakers, researchers, and stakeholders.

With interactive visualizations, detailed trend analysis, and comprehensive case studies, our platform not only tracks climate variability but also correlates it with agricultural productivity and extreme weather events.
